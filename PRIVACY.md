# Privacy Policy — Status Helper

_Last updated: 2026-06-07_

Status Helper is designed to respect your privacy. In short: **everything happens
on your device, and nothing about your videos ever leaves it.**

## What the app does

Status Helper converts and trims videos so they can be posted as a status on
WhatsApp and similar apps. All processing (reading the video, converting it, and
saving the result) runs **locally on your device** using an on-device version of
FFmpeg.

## Data we collect

**None.** Status Helper does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal
information. Specifically:

- It has **no analytics**, no advertising, and no tracking.
- It does **not** send your videos, or any data about them, to any server.
- It does **not** require an account or internet connection to function.

## Permissions the app uses

- **Read videos / photos** (`READ_MEDIA_VIDEO`, `READ_MEDIA_IMAGES`, and on older
  Android versions `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`): needed only to read the video you
  explicitly pick or share into the app so it can be converted.
- **Save to gallery**: the converted video is written to a "StatusHelper" album in
  your gallery so you can post it.

The app only ever touches the specific video you choose or share with it. It does
not browse, upload, or index your media library.

## Where your files go

Converted videos are saved to your device's gallery (a "StatusHelper" album) and,
when you tap Share, handed to the app you choose via the system share sheet. The
app never uploads them anywhere itself.
